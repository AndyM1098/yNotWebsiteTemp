OUR MISSION STATEMENT

Here at YNOT, we strive to bring teams together through the joy of engineering. In our four years as a team, we have mentored hundreds of students, hosted tournaments, and volunteered across the American South.


Our ability to work together, fostering teamwork and a vigorous exchange of ideas, allows us to succeed. We work tirelessly to improve our robots through the Engineering Design Process. Our team members learn to build, code, perform complex CAD designs, 3D print materials, and machine parts for our robots.


Our team is associated with the Tickle College of Engineering at the University of Tennessee Knoxville, and our team members use the work we perform in lab to supplement their engineering education. While helping each other grow, we strive to do the very best we can to create quality, innovative robots, and have fun while we're doing it; because after all... YNOT.
